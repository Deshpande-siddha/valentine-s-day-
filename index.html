Happy Rose Day!

In a world where treasures are few,

I'd still find a way, just for you,

To plant a garden, lush and bright,

With roses blooming, day and night.


Even if my pockets were bare,

I'd scatter petals everywhere,

To see you smile, even for a while,

Makes every effort worthwhile.


For in your smile, my love, I find,

A garden of roses in my mind,

So I'd give all I have, gladly so,

To make your heart dance and glow.
